Why Web Design for Civic and Social Responsibility important

Definition of Responsibilities

Before we go into any detailed information, it is important to know the definition of the two responsibilities that this post is going to talk about.

  1. Civic Responsibility: This can be defined as the "responsibility of a citizen”. It can be described as an action that individual makes that is related to government, church, and volunteers. From those actions, others get encouraged to participate any related actions.
  2. Social responsibility: The idea that businesses should balance profit-making activities with activities that benefit society. It involves developing businesses with a positive relationship to the society in which they operate. It can be something little, like recycling, to big, which can influence whole world. This responsibility usually happen in big business, showing how the customer’s money and love are going into.

Why it is so important?

Civic and social responsibility has become increasingly important to companies. Whether it is for helping the environment or solving poverty problem, more and more companies are incorporating social responsibility into their overall business strategy. By adding those responsibilities, they not only improve the company’s brand, but also allow to build engaging relationship with customers. The company’s social responsibility can show the true perception of the company, which provide trust from customers. When there are customers who trust the company, they will allow company to stand out from other related companies.

Why we need civic and social responsibility in web

In now days, people web search in every area. Whenever people have question about how company or any social group run, behave, or have impact to society, environment, and world, they usually search online to find more information and resolve their curiosity. Since the world is very media-centered, it is important for company or group to provide information about their civic and social responsibility. If there is no website for any business or group, people assume they don’t exist. In order to communicate with people and population, any business, especially the ones that include social responsibility have to have website that has sufficient information and easy guide, so people can easily find the information that they are looking for.
